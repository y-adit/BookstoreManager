About Bookstore Manager

Welcome to the Bookstore Manager, a comprehensive full-stack solution designed to bridge the gap between digital book discovery and local store availability.

How It Works

  • Explore the Catalog: Our system uses advanced data simulation to provide a vast library of titles and authors to browse.
  • Compare Stores: Unlike a standard shop, this app scans multiple simulated vendors to find you the best price and fastest delivery time for any specific book.
  • Lock & Secure: Once you find the perfect deal, you "Lock" the selection. This immediately updates the store's inventory in real-time, ensuring that the book is reserved for you.

This platform streamlines the connection between readers and retailers, ensuring transparent pricing and accurate stock levels across all participating locations.
